    About Reflecto

    Your private, server-side productivity companion. Built with Python + Flask, with everything saved as local JSON files on your own machine.

    🌤

    Mood Tracking

    Log how you're feeling once a day with a single tap. Reflecto quietly keeps your streak and a recent history so you can look back and notice patterns over time.

    ✍

    Journal

    A distraction-free writing space for your thoughts. Create, edit, and revisit entries whenever you need to think out loud or simply put the day into words.

    📚

    Study Notes

    Organize notes by category, pin the ones you reach for often, and search through everything instantly. Export any note as CSV whenever you need a copy.

    ✅

    Tasks

    Keep a simple to-do list with priority flags and due dates. Reflecto flags anything overdue automatically and tracks your overall completion progress.

    ⏱

    Timer & Alarm

    A built-in countdown timer, stopwatch with laps, and alarms — handy for Pomodoro-style focus sessions or just keeping time on a task.

    🎨

    Themes

    Pick from a dozen light and dark palettes — from calm blues and greens to warm browns and purples — and Reflecto remembers your choice next time.

    FAQ

    Where is my data stored?
    Everything you write — moods, journal entries, notes, and tasks — is saved as a local JSON file on the machine running Reflecto. Nothing is sent to a server or stored in the cloud.
    Is my data shared or tracked in any way?
    No. Reflecto has no analytics, ads, or tracking of any kind. Your entries stay between you and your own files.
    Can I export my notes or data?
    Yes. You can export any individual study note as a CSV file, or export your full account as a PDF from the sidebar.
    What happens if I reset my data?
    Resetting clears your journal, notes, tasks, and mood history for a fresh start. Your theme preference is kept. This action can't be undone, so Reflecto asks you to confirm first.
    Can more than one person use Reflecto?
    Yes. Each person signs in with their own name and password, and gets their own private space and data file — switch users any time from the sidebar.
    Do I need an internet connection to use it?
    No, Reflecto runs entirely on your machine. An internet connection is only used for the optional contact form.
